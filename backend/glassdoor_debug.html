          Lead I - Software Engineering(java Developer(Trivandrum))

          Thiruvananthapuram

          Senior Java Developer

          Experience: 5+ Years

          Location: Trivandrum & Kochi

          Mandatory skills: Java, springboot, microservices, AWS/GCP

          Key Responsibilities & Requirements

          • 5+ years of hands-on experience in designing and developing enterprise solutions using Java/J2EE following multi-tier architecture.
          • Strong proficiency in frameworks such as Spring, Spring Boot, Spring Cloud, and Hibernate.
          • Demonstrated proficiency in architectural principles and design patterns for enterprise solutions.
          • Hands-on experience developing solutions in one or more cloud environments: AWS, GCP, or Azure (mandatory).
          • Good knowledge of cloud-native compute, storage, and networking services (preferred).
          • Strong understanding of APIs, Web Services, Microservices, and familiarity with API Management Gateways.
          • Hands-on experience with RDBMS such as Oracle, MySQL, or PostgreSQL.
          • Knowledge of NoSQL databases is desirable.
          • Solid understanding of CI/CD concepts and experience with tools such as:
          • Jenkins, SonarQube, Nexus, Git, Maven, Gradle, Rundeck
          • Strong understanding of security principles across all phases of the SDLC, including code security.
          • Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, high-volume, deadline-driven environment.
          • Proven ability to lead teams of 1015 members to design, develop, and deploy enterprise solutions to production.
          • Experience mentoring team members, guiding them to build high-quality, scalable solutions, and troubleshooting issues.
          • Experience analyzing user stories, providing effort estimates, and ensuring timely delivery as a team.
          • Hands-on experience across the full project lifecycle, from requirements gathering to production support.
          • Ability to learn and adapt quickly to new transformation technology stacks.
          • Strong exposure to Agile and Scaled Agile (SAFe) development methodologies.

          Skills

          java,spring boot,microservices,ci/cd,jenkins,

          Job Type: Full-time

          Work Location: In person
